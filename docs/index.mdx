**Fqtrader** is the trading system in the [`ftbot/`](https://github.com/saifbelaarbi/trader-google-c) directory.
[Freqtrade](https://www.freqtrade.io) is the execution engine; Claude is the research and
operations layer. The active strategy, **`ClaudeBreakout`**, runs 24/7 in dry-run
(paper) mode and is graduating to real money behind hard gates.

<Note>
  **Paper-trading in progress.** The bot trades dry-run only. No real money moves until
  every [go-live gate](/go-live) passes. Nothing here changes the live strategy mid-test.
</Note>

## The strategy in one line

A turtle-style **Donchian breakout**: enter on a 20-bar high/low break filtered by EMA200,
exit on the opposite 10-bar extreme, hard stop at 2×ATR. 9 USDT-perp pairs, max 4 open,
$200 dry-run wallet, 4h candles.

## How judgments are made

This is a **low-win, high-payoff** system — win rate is a red herring. Strategies are judged
on **profit factor** and **Calmar** (return ÷ drawdown), **net of fees and funding**, never on
win rate. A profit factor of 1.15 at a 31% win rate implies an average winner ~2.5× the
average loser; the whole game is protecting that payoff ratio while surviving long losing
streaks.
